
## 🎯 YouTube Video ID Guide
1. Go to your YouTube video
2. Copy the video URL
3. Extract the ID after `v=`
   
**Example:**
- URL: `https://youtube.com/watch?v=ABC123def456`  
- Video ID: `ABC123def456`
